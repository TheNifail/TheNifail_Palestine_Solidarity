Open Your Eyes

We stand in solidarity with the Palestinian people, who have for far too long endured displacement, hardship, and the denial of their fundamental rights. We recognize their resilience and unwavering pursuit of self-determination. We call for an end to the ongoing injustices, the occupation, and the violence, and we reaffirm our commitment to a just and lasting peace that respects the dignity and human rights of all Palestinians. We believe that freedom and justice for Palestine are essential for peace and stability in the region and the world.
